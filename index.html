The Story

The lobby gets the budget. The sachet gets forgotten.

Now imagine the other version. The sugar a guest stirs into their coffee, the cutlery they unwrap, the bottle on the table — each one carrying your name, quietly turning an ordinary meal into a memory of your brand. That's the impact a fully branded pantry creates.

These weren't overlooked out of carelessness. Custom packaging had a reputation for huge minimum orders, confusing print specs, and suppliers who only talked to big chains. It simply wasn't accessible.

BrandMyHoReCa was built to change that. One supplier, six essential products, the lowest MOQ in the market, and a process simple enough to take you from logo to doorstep in one conversation.

Your packaging deserves the same care as your cooking. We make that possible, at any scale.
